# Account FAQ

## How to Register?

Open the homepage and click "Register", then fill in your email and password.

## Forgot Password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset via email verification code.

## Can I Change My Email?

Currently, email changes are not supported. Please make sure your email is correct during registration.

## Will My Account Be Deleted?

Accounts that remain inactive for a long time may be deleted. We recommend logging in at least once every six months.

## Can I Register Multiple Accounts?

Please follow the principle of good faith: one person, one account. Abuse will result in a ban.
