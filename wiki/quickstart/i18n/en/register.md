# Registration & Login

## Register an Account

1. Open the Passnux homepage
2. Click the "Register" button in the top-right corner
3. Fill in your email address and password
4. Click "Register" to complete

## Log In

1. Click the "Login" button
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Login" to enter the main interface

## Password Reset

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset via email verification code.

## Notes

- Use a real email address to receive important notifications
- Password must be at least 8 characters long
- It is recommended to include letters, numbers, and special characters
