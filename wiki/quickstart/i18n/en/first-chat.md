# Your First Chat

## Entering the Chat

After logging in, you will see the chat interface. The left side shows the conversation list, and the right side is the chat area.

## Sending a Message

1. Type your question in the input box at the bottom
2. Press Enter or click the send button
3. The AI will reply to your message in real time

## Selecting a Model

You can switch between different AI models at the top of the chat interface:

- **DeepSeek** — General conversation, fast
- **GPT-3.5** — Strong English capability
- **Tongyi Qianwen** — Good Chinese understanding

## Tips

- The clearer your question, the more accurate the answer
- Multi-turn conversations are supported; the AI remembers context
- Click "Clear Chat" to start a new topic
